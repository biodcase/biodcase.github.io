Instructions for Presenters

The DCASE2024 Workshop has lecture session and poster session. We will announce the presentation type to authors very soon.

Lecture presentation

Each presentation time slot is 20 minutes including lectures, discussions (questions and answers), and handovers to the next presenter. The presenter is expected to have their presentation with 15 minutes and leave 5 minutes for questions, answers, and handover to the next presenter. It is highly recommended that the presenter checks that slides are correctly displayed on the screen before your session starts.

Poster preparation

Posters should have A0 size and be in portrait orientation. There is no template and there are no special requirements regarding the content beyond what is usually expected from a scientific poster. The presenter must mount their poster before the session starts.
