Talks

 

Each presentation is allocated 20 minutes. The talk itself must not exceed 15 minutes. The remaining five minutes are reserved for questions. Please make sure that your talk keeps to the time limit by practising it at least once under realistic conditions.

 

Posters

 

Posters should have A0 size and be in portrait orientation (width x height: 84.1 cm x 118.9 cm / 33.1 in x 46.8 in). There is no template and there are no special requirements regarding the content beyond what is usually expected from a scientific poster.
