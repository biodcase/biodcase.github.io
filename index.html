BioDCASE - Evaluation & Benchmarking in Automated Bioacoustics

BioDCASE is a new initiative focused on advancing research in computational bioacoustics through annual challenges and workshops, modelled after the successful format of the DCASE challenges. By providing benchmark datasets and tasks specific to bioacoustic applications, BioDCASE aims to foster innovation and collaboration within the community.
