Workshop Registration

Registration

 

The DCASE2018 Workshop will take place on Monday, 19th November, and Tuesday, 20th November, at the WWF Living Planet Centre in Woking, Surrey, UK.

 

Registration is closed now and the workshop sold out.

 

Authors: At least one author for each paper must register by 1st October 2018.

Submitting a paper or a contribution to the challenge is not a condition for participation in the workshop: Everyone can attend.

 

The fees are as follows:

Participant Early Registration (by 20th October) Regular Registration (by 1st November)
Standard £200 £250
Student* £150 £200

* For Registration at the Student rate, please send a letter confirming your student status from your supervisor or institution via email to: dcase.workshop@gmail.com

 

The registration fee includes entrance to the workshop, coffee/tea breaks, buffet lunch (daily) and the Welcome Reception on Monday 19th November.

We can supply a visa letter if required by registered delegates. To request a visa letter, please e-mail dcase.workshop@gmail.com.
