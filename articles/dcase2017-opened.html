DCASE2017 Opened

Fri 03 March 2017, Toni Heittola
dcase

The DCASE2017 website now public. This website collects all the information about the DCASE2017 Challenge and DCASE2017 Workshop.

Challenge

Development datasets and the baseline system for the Challenge will be released on 15th of March. Until then, there might still be minor modifications to the task definitions.

Workshop

This year the workshop will be extended to one-and-half day workshop. We are currently working on the organization of the workshop. Invited speakers will be announced closer to the workshop, as well as the detailed schedule.
